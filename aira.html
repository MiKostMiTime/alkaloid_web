Aira is an idol who is also a big fan of other idols. He is a quarter French because his mother is half French and half Japanese. Aira is amiable, outgoing, and brave. He is also able to face reality with sound judgment. His singing voice is innocent and boyish, and during his performances, his cuteness always leaks out, which adds more adorableness to his stage.

 

Aira is the member of ALKALOID at STARMAKER PRODUCTION.
